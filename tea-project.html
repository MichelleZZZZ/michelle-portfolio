About the
project

This is a site about tea, for instance, the healthy benefits and caffeine levels in different kinds of tea. I collected as much more information as I could, also went to some teashops in Auckland, meanwhile I made a survey on SurveyMonkey.

UI / UX / HTML+CSS / Sass / jQuery / WordPress

UX & UI
Design Process

Survey

I made a survey which included ten questions and 12 people joined the survey

Content Hierarchy

Based on the feedbacks from the survey I sketched the site map and wireframes for phone size, also marked the animation places out.

Wireframes Mock-up

I mocked up the wireframes for phone ,tablet and desktop size.

WordPress / Content Management

I rebuilt this site on WordPress and uploaded the images and texts on WordPress. Anyone who use this site could change the image or texts on WordPress dashboard. It`s a good practice to know the content management.
